French Toast Bake

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Raisin Bread 2 loaves
Eggs 9
Maple Syrup 3/4 cup
Milk 3 3/4 cup
Cinnamon 1 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Additional Items
10" x 15" pan
Wisk
Measuring cups

Step 1: Place 2-3 layers of bread in the pan.

Step 2: Wisk together the eggs, syrup, salt, and milk. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Step 3: Pour the mixture evenly over the bread. Sprinkle cinnamon over top.

Step 4: Put the pan in the oven for 20-30 mins or until golden, checking to make sure it doesn't burn.

Step 5: Serve with frozen or fresh fruit and syrup.

Adapted from Quickies' recipe.

Apple-cheddar-wich

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 8 slices
Green Apples 2
Cheddar Cheese
Mayonnaise
Mustard (optional)
Additional Items
Cookie Sheet
Cutting Board

Step 1: Lightly toast the bread.

Step 2: Spread a layer of mayonnaise on four(4) pieces of bread. Optional: Spread mustard on the bread, too.

Step 3: Slice the cheese, enough to make a layer on each of the four slices, and place them on the bread.

Step 4: Thinly slice the apples and put 2-3 slices on the four(4) pieces of bread.

Step 5: Close the sandwiches and put the pan with the sandwiches in the oven on broil until the cheese is melted, checking to make sure it doesn't burn.

Mashed Potatoes

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
White Potatoes 3 Small
Green Onion 1 Branch
Mayonnaise 1 Large Scoop
Black Pepper 7 Twists
Dill Generous Amount
Optional:
Green Beans
Any Personal Favourites
Additional Items
Bowl
Cutting Board

Step 1: Mince the green onion and dill.

Step 2: Add green onion and potatoes in the bowl and mash them with a fork.

Step 3: Add black pepper, mayonnaise, dill, and any personal fovourites before mashing to your prefered consistency.
